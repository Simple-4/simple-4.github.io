FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Narbeth's Simple Learning Academy 4.0.

How do I enroll at Narbeth's Academy?+
Simply complete our online application form and submit the required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, passport photo, and parent/guardian ID). Our admissions team will guide you through each step of the process.
Do you offer online learning options?+
Selected courses, particularly in our Skills Development program, may include online or hybrid learning options. Please contact our admissions office for details on which programs currently offer remote learning arrangements.
Are scholarships or financial assistance available?+
Scholarships may be available based on academic achievement or financial need. We are committed to making quality education accessible. Please reach out to our administrative office for current scholarship availability and application criteria.
How can parents monitor their child's progress?+
Parents can communicate directly with teachers through scheduled meetings and our parent-teacher conferences. We provide regular progress reports each term, and our staff is always available to discuss your child's development and any areas of concern.
What are the school hours?+
Our administrative office is open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM. Standard class hours may vary by program. Please contact us for the specific schedule for your program of interest.
What extracurricular activities are available?+
We offer a rich variety of activities including academic competitions, science projects, reading programs, computer club, debate club, art club, music club, sports activities, and community outreach — ensuring every student finds their passion beyond the classroom.
